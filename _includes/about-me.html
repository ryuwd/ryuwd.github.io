I am a Postgraduate (PhD) researcher at the University of Edinburgh Particle Physics Experiment Group, working on the LHCb Experiment based at the Large Hadron Collider, CERN.

Until September 2020, I was a Postgraduate MSc Research student at the University of Manchester, where I worked on Detector Alignment for the Mu2e Experiment at Fermilab.

A lot of my work involves the analysis of large quantities of data e.g. acquired by the LHCb detector. I enjoy programming and regularly contribute to software, where I can. My GitHub and my CERN GitLab profiles display some of my recent contributions.

2020-now Charm spectroscopy analyses at LHCb
